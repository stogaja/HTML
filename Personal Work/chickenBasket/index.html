Who are we?

Chicken Basket is a social enterprise that works with women and youths in poultry production as a source of livelihood. We are a one-stop shop. In this model, our farmers get chicks feeds and medications all under one roof. In the process of production, the farmers also enjoy support services, free training and extension services from our vet. The farmers also enjoy market guarantee for their grown chickens. We collect back the chicken, we process and then we sell to hotels, restaurants and the market within our reach.
